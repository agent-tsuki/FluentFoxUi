## Concept 01: Existence (あります vs います)
Japanese uses two different verbs for "to be" or "to exist" depending on whether the subject is animate (living, moving) or inanimate.

| Verb | Usage | Examples |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **あります** (arimasu) | Non-living things, plants, abstract concepts. | Computer, park, meeting, flower. |
| **います** (imasu) | Humans and animals (sentient beings). | Teacher, student, dog, cat. |



---

## Concept 02: Stating Location (Place に N が あります/います)
Use this pattern to say "In [Place], there is [Noun]". The location is marked by the particle **に**.

* **わたしの部屋（へや）に 机（つくえ）が あります。**
    * *Watashi no heya ni tsukue ga arimasu.*
    * In my room, there is a desk.
* **事務所（じむしょ）に 佐藤（さとう）さんが います。**
    * *Jimusho ni Satō-san ga imasu.*
    * In the office, there is Mr. Sato.

---

## Concept 03: Describing Noun Position (N は Place に あります/います)
Use this pattern to say "[Noun] is in [Place]". This focuses on the specific location of a known topic.

* **東京（とうきょう）ディズニーランドは 千葉県（ちばけん）に あります。**
    * *Tōkyō Dizunīrando wa Chiba-ken ni arimasu.*
    * Tokyo Disneyland is in Chiba Prefecture.
* **家族（かぞく）は ニューヨークに います。**
    * *Kazoku wa Nyūyōku ni imasu.*
    * My family is in New York.

---

## Concept 04: Relative Location (N₁ [Place] の N₂ [Position])
To describe specific placement (on, under, inside), use the particle **の** between the place and the position noun.

* **机（つくえ）の上（うえ）** — On the desk.
* **箱（はこ）の中（なか）** — Inside the box.
* **郵便局（ゆうびんきょく）の隣（となり）** — Next to the post office.



### Interrogative: Where is it?
* **駅（えき）は どこに ありますか。**
    * *Eki wa doko ni arimasu ka.*
    * Where is the station?

---

## Concept 05: The Inclusive Particle (N₁ や N₂ [など])
While **と** links an exhaustive list, **や** is used to list two or three items as representatives of a larger group (meaning "N1, N2, and so on"). **など** is often added at the end of the list for further emphasis.

* **箱（はこ）の中に 手紙（てがみ）や 写真（しゃしん）が あります。**
    * *Hako no naka ni tegami ya shashin ga arimasu.*
    * In the box, there are letters, photos, and so on.

---

## Sidebar: Reference & Nuance

### Confirming Information: 〜ね
Use **ね** at the end of a sentence to confirm a location or seek agreement.
* **すみません、ユニーバサル・スタジオ・ジャパンは どこに ありますか。**
* **...大阪（おおさか）に ありますよ。**
* **大阪ですね。ありがとうございます。** (Osaka, right. Thank you.)
